About Our Candle Business

Welcome to Flame to Fable, a handcrafted candle brand based in Kerala crafted by Christina Roseline. We create the best budget-friendly premium handmade candles in Kerala that transform any space into a sanctuary of warmth and peace. Each candle is thoughtfully handcrafted using quality wax, soothing fragrances, and refined aesthetic details - perfect for homes, gifting, return gifts, and special occasions.

🎨 NEW! Now offering Design Your Own Candles service - Create personalized candles with your choice of colors, fragrances, shapes, and designs. Perfect for special events, corporate gifts, return gifts, or expressing your unique style!

Our collection features eco-friendly soy candles, gel candles, scented candles, festival-special designs, and custom-designed candles - all crafted with care in God's own country, Kerala. Whether you want perfect gifts, return gifts for functions, or beautiful décor pieces, our candles are designed to make every moment memorable.
